Air pollution, meteorological conditions and respiratory infections in Baix Llobregat: a 14-year analysis

The objectives of this research project are:

  • David & Marc are studying all data from Gavà, a city located in Baix Llobregat county. In this county we are using data from Air Pollution Prevention and Monitoring Network (XVPCA) . Data correspond to hourly air pollution measurements from 1991 to 2025. Included variables: Nitrogen oxide (NO), nitrogen dioxide (NO₂), nitrogen oxides (NOx), ozone (O₃) and solar irradiance.
  • Respiratory infection data are collected from the SIVIC server using data from region 75 (Barcelona Metropolitan South). Region 201 is the identifier of the Primary Care Centre from Gavà.
  • Meteorological data is available from Meteocat (Meteorological data server of Catalonia). We selected the nearest meteorological station: El Prat de Llobregat. The data structure is based on semi-hourly data (every 30 minutes) from 2009. Variables include: temperature, relative humidity, pressure, wind direction, wind speed and solar irradiance.
